Background

After attending an all boys boarding school in Connecticut, I spent my late teens and early twenties attending West Point for a short period, serving in the Marine Corps, and honing my craft as a strength and conditioning coach. In 2019, after my military service came to an end, I started Delta Five Performance, a strength and conditioning service built to serve elite athletes on their journey to success. After a few years, I eventually opened a state of the art training facility in Avon, CO called MTN Barbell with two other business partners.

Delta Five Performance

Delta Five Performance, the first company I created, has reached thousands of a thletes and coaches with our services. We offer training and consulting services to anyone seeking to obtain excellence in a particular task. Whether it be successfully passing a rigorous military selection course, lifting 400lbs overhead in a strongman competition, or losing body fat for a wedding, our results speak for themselves. By October of 2022, Delta Five has played a major role in the success of a World Champion Jiu-Jitsu athlete, Army Rangers, Marine Infantry Officers, Green Berets, Army Sappers, Professional Strongmen, a PGA Golfer, former NFL athletes, former Olympians, NCAA Division 1 Athletes, and many, many more.

MTN Barbell

MTN Barbell is a uniquely special facility located in Avon, Colorado, just west of the more famous Vail. Stacked with some of the best equipment in the world, there are very few lines of tissue, if any at all, that you couldn't train with an optimal stimulus inside our walls. Our low volume membership model allows for MTN Barbell to exist as a private training facility for anyone lucky enough to have a spot. Combined with the best training staff in the Vail Valley, you would be hard pressed to find a better option to turn your dreams into reality.
